Standing Out From The Crowd – We Think Differently – That is Well.

Gandow Law LLP is a niche business law firm, we look to put in place well thought out commercial strategies for our clients. We are creative thinkers that help clients attain their goals. We make the most complex matters simple for you to understand. We are transparent in and how we charge. We like to sit down with you and discuss the ins and outs of what’s going on in your business.

What are the three key things that sets us apart from our competitors?

1.Superior Industry Knowledge

Our business is centred around the key business sectors in Ireland. We have been working that way for over 25 years. Our clients are always changing and looking for new challenges and our solicitors have been involved over the years specialising in these sectors and getting first hand experience of some of the largest and most complex transactions in these sectors. We have worked on ground breaking cases in the healthcare, energy, technology, media and financial services to name but a few. We have also been involved in many complex commercial transactions in the food and agri-business, retail, commercial property and manufacturing sectors.

2.Focused Client Services

Online Client Services

If required, we provide an online service to clients hereby they can access key information in relation to their files without having to pick up the telephone to us. We can tailor this service to your requirements. It is extremely easy to access and to use.

What kind of things can we do for you? We can set up online data rooms, you can monitor work in progress, key dates of litigation or a commercial transaction, access financial information such as invoices to name but a few functions, all within a secure environment.

We can allow you have secure access to certain documents and create a diary with key dates and reminders. The applications are endless depending on what particular information you want and how you want it delivered.

Cost management service

We believe in transparency. Let’s get to the point, we want you as a client in the future, we want to work together with you as your business grows. You need to plan for the future, and you need to know exactly how much things cost. We can provide you with the forecasts and monitoring of legal costs that you need through our advanced cost management system.

3.Lateral Thinking

Great minds most definitely discuss ideas. It is hugely important to us that we continually look at novel and creative ways of solving problems. We like to sit down and talk through different scenarios and to come up with the best solution for our clients. Our solicitors have the innate ability to look at a problem from a number of different perspectives, and to provide our clients with a range of potential solutions. We also like to look at the risks involved, the associated costs and the probability of success. Very often the answer is directly in front of you, but it is the well thought out strategy that will guide a client to success.
