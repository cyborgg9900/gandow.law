Smart Practice

You might call it success or simply solving a problem. Always eager to help, we are just as good at tackling the smaller issues as we are at solving the bigger problems. Here are some recent legal transactions from our clients and team.

Why have so many businesses come to Gandow Law LLP for advice? Because working together is what success is all about. Have a look below, at some of the transactions we have been involved in, we are always looking to get the very best out of, very often, extremely difficult situations. We are commercial, practical, clever and above all we listen to our clients. We are proud of . Why not try Gandow Law LLP, your corporate and commercial legal team?

For corporate transactions we give structuring advice, we provide non-disclosure agreements, we advise on term sheets, we carry out due diligence, we draft and negotiate transactional documents, we are experts in deal management and completing transactions and we help sourcing specialists and advisors.

For litigation we give strategic and commercial advice, we provide pre-litigation and pre-trial advice, we carry out discovery, we draft Court papers and brief Counsel, we are experts in managing the progress of your case and we are experts in alternative dispute resolution, we negotiate and draft settlement agreements, but above all we look to compromise cases to the complete satisfaction of clients at the right time and in a manner that gets the best result for them and their business.

As you can see, we have done a whole range of transactions for both Irish and International clients. Each transaction is important to us, whether it is small or large. If there is a challenge, we take it on as if it is our own. What is so great about these transactions? Well, for us it is about happy clients, and clients that come back to us time and again because they are absolutely comfortable that we deliver outstanding legal services.
