We provide resources for business

Legal Resources – Services

We are looking to redefine legal services. In fact, in the legal world, it is all about service. To this end we want to share with you some key resources that will help your business grow. We want to add to the legal knowledge base. Our input and drafting skills can then be used to specifically tailor any of these documents to your particular requirements. Feel free to have a look through our online legal resources. Of course, you might want to use one of these documents for your business. Give us a call and we would be more than happy to advise you or specifically tailor a document to your liking.

Legal Documents

All businesses should be equipped with a broad range of legal documents that underpin their business. We hope to develop our resources over the coming months and to share with you even more legal information and resources.

Whether it is corporate law, commercial law, litigation and business disputes. Or maybe it is property transactions and disputes, mergers and acquisitions, cross-border trade and disputes, European Law, Planning and environmental law. Or finally banking and finance law, energy law, healthcare law, sports law, debt recovery, product liability law or commercial contracts. We have a wealth of knowledge to share with you.

Resources

We are well attuned to working with clients on high volume projects. Of course, we can specifically draft, for example, fixed term and rolling employment contracts. In addition, we can assist you in training your staff in order to ensure that they know the ins and outs of completing employment contracts. Our resources are the first step to helping you reduce risk in your business. Come and talk to Gandow Law LLP if you need to practical advice. Feel free to use our resources as a starting point to understanding how to protect your business and how to make it grow.
