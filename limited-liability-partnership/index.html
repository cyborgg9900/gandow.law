Limited Liability Partnership

As and from 1 February 2021, Gandow Law LLP will operate as a limited liability partnership, established in the Republic of Ireland.  Gandow Law LLP is regulated by the Law Society Ireland and authorised by the Legal Services Regulatory Authority to operate as limited liability partnership under the Legal Services Regulation Act 2015.

As set out in the Legal Services Regulation Act 2015, (subject to the exceptions listed therein), a Partner in Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland, has no personal liability for any debts, liabilities or obligations which are incurred for the purpose of carrying on the business of Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland (whether these are liabilities of Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland, of himself or herself, of another partner/member or partners or members in Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland or of any employee, agent or representative of Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland) and however such liability may arise.

The above relates only to the personal liability of Partners and does not prevent or restrict the enforcement against the property of Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland of any debt, liability or obligation.  The Partnership Act 1890 (Ireland) applies to Gandow Law LLP registered in Ireland to the extent that it is not inconsistent with Chapter 3 of Part 8 of the Legal Services Regulation Act 2015.
